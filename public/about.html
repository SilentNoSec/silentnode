About

Privacy is not a feature. It is a right.

Mission

SilentNode exists to provide secure, anonymous communication infrastructure. Privacy should be the default, not the exception.

Every system we build operates on a zero-knowledge principle: we do not collect, process, or store any data about our users. What we do not have, we cannot lose, and we cannot be compelled to hand over.

Principles

  • Zero collection — No logs, no cookies, no tracking, no analytics
  • End-to-end encryption — All communication is encrypted before it leaves your device
  • No identity required — Use our services without revealing who you are
  • Open verification — Our security claims are verifiable, not promises
  • Tor-first design — Full functionality via hidden service for maximum anonymity

Threat Model

We design against adversaries ranging from passive network observers to state-level actors. Our infrastructure is distributed, payment-anonymous, and jurisdiction-resilient.

No system is perfect. We are transparent about limitations and continuously harden our defenses.
